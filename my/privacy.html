Privacy Policy

At Straight to the Hire, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with global privacy regulations.

Data Collection and Use

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and company details when you register for our services or submit forms on our platform. This data is used to provide and improve our services, including hiring, onboarding, and payroll management for global teams. We may also collect usage data, such as IP addresses and browsing behavior, to enhance user experience and ensure platform security.

Your information is processed to fulfill contractual obligations, comply with legal requirements, and communicate updates about our services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors) who adhere to strict privacy standards and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Data Protection and Your Rights

Straight to the Hire adheres to GDPR, SOC2, and ISO 27001 standards to protect your data. We use encryption, secure servers hosted by AWS, and annual third-party audits to ensure robust security. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, as well as to restrict or object to its processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. If you choose to close your account, we will delete your personal information unless required to retain it by law. Updates to this Privacy Policy will be posted on our website, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.
